Alkaline Oreos

Description

Here is a snack that will satisfy your cravings and wanting you to make some more for the whole family!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups spelt flour
  • 1/4 cup grapeseed oil
  • 2 tbsp tahini
  • 1/3 cup date syrup
  • pinch sea salt
  • pack of coconut cream (200g)>
  • 100 ml (0.42 cups) spring water
  • 4 tbsp agave syrup

Steps

  1. Mix dough together and cut into oreo size pieces. Remember 2 cookies create 1 oreo.
  2. Bake cookies for 15 min.
  3. Mix cream together and create oreo sandwiches with baked cookies. Let set for 1 hour and enjoy.
