Alkaline Chickpea Tuna

Description

This dish will re-imagine the way we think about having seafood without the fish in our dish!

Ingredients

  • 1-2 cups cooked chickpeas
  • diced onions
  • 2 tbsp tahini
  • olive oil
  • pinch sea salt
  • kelp/dulse sea seasoning>
  • spring water
  • key lime juice

Steps

  1. Mash chickpeas with fork until a tuna/fish like appears is created.
  2. Add dry seasonings first and mix then follow by adding diced onions.
  3. Finish by adding tahini, olive, oil, & key lime juice to this mix. If you want to have a thinner texture add some water. Enjoy!
