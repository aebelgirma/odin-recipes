Alkaline Burro Banana Fries

Description

Burro Banana Fries are arguably the best substitute for potatoes for this dish. Unripe burro bananas hardly even taste like bananas at all and are so deceptive that it's guilt-free!

This side dish is common in countries close to the equator and East Africa as well.

As you prep your first green burro banana, you'll want to start planning trips to the nearest coastal city not just surrounded by palm trees.

Ingredients

  • Green/Unripe Burro Bananas
  • Avocado Oil
  • Sea Salt
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Peel bananas and slice length-wise into fry shape.
  2. Spray or pour moderate amount of avocado oil that enough to coat all of the fries.
  3. Add some sea salt and generous amount of basil to your desired amount.
  4. Cook it to 400F for 10-15 minutes.
